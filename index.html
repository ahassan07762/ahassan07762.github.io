  • Accessibility and Travel

    Our core belief is that travel is a force for good. Travel enriches those who are touched by it either directly or indirectly. Travelling with a disability requires a lot of organisation, but information on accessibility is often hard to find. Around 50% of people with a disability would travel more if they could be sure more accessible facilities were available. We believe it’s important to ensure that individuals with a disability have more access to travel opportunities.
